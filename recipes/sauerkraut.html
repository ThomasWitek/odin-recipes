Sauerkraut Recipe

Pickled Cabbage

Great way to store cabbage for the long winter, tastes great in a German Chocolate cake as well as on top of a frank.

Ingredients:

  • Sauerkraut
  • Caraway Seeds
  • Juniper Berries
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Drain Sauerkraut and squeeze rinse out to desired tartness.
  2. Oil in pan and cook on medium-low with Caraway seeds and Juniper berries covered.
  3. Good job.
